## Description

A composite chart of {{the-shattered-reach}}, stitched from a dozen conflicting soundings. It marks the anchorage at {{kells-hollow}}, the reef-mazes where {{captain-ferocious}} was last seen, and the shipping lanes patrolled by {{house-veldrane}}.

## Recent History

The chart is redrawn after every storm — the Reach does not hold still. This edition postdates {{the-sundering}} by two generations and is already out of date in its northern quarter.
