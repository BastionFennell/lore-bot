## Description

They call her Powderkeg for the burn-scars that map one side of her face and for her temper, which is worse. She commands the ironclad *Brazen Debt* and speaks softly only when someone is about to be keelhauled.

## Recent History

Powderkeg planned and led the seizure of {{kells-hollow}} on behalf of {{house-veldrane}}. She has sworn — publicly, before witnesses — an oath to hang {{captain-ferocious}} from her own mainmast.

## Relationships

- Admiral of {{house-veldrane}}.
- Sworn enemy of {{captain-ferocious}}.
- Holds {{kells-hollow}} under martial tariff.
