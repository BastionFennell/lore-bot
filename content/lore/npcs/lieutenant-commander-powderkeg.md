## Description

An aggressive, sharkish woman who wields bombs and grenades as her signature weapons. She has little care for collateral damage.

## Role

Leader of the {{bombadiers}} and captain of the {{black-powder}}. Her disregard for collateral damage made it hard for her to climb the Navy ranks, but her aggressive capture of pirate-controlled ports and her ability to track down and decimate whole fleets forced Naval leadership to recognize and reward her strength through promotion and assignment.

## Recent History

She has recently taken control of {{zelda}}'s inn, the {{tide-and-travel}}, and intends to turn Zelda and {{driftwood}} in to the Navy for a modest reward.
