## Description

House Veldrane is old money with new cannons. Once a mainland shipping dynasty, the Sundering left its estates underwater and its ambitions intact. It now rules from a flotilla of ironclads, trading in tariffs, powder, and fear across {{the-shattered-reach}}.

## Recent History

Under {{captain-powderkeg}} the House seized {{kells-hollow}} and its customs house, imposing the tariff that sparked the Mutiny. Its ships fly the black-and-brass ensign, and its debtors sign the {{the-sundering}}-era oaths the House still enforces at gunpoint.
