## Description

The Sundering is the event that ends every old story and begins every new one. In a single tide the sea-continent cracked, the mountains became islands, and the old kingdoms went under with their crowns still on. Scholars argue whether it was a curse, a weapon, or a debt come due.

## Recent History

Two generations later, the waters have calmed but not forgiven. The wreckage became {{the-shattered-reach}}, and the survivors became captains. The oath-magic of the drowned era — the binding vows sworn on iron — still holds, which is why houses like {{house-veldrane}} can enforce debts older than anyone living.
