## Description

Mara Quillon reads currents the way other people read faces. Raised on the gangplanks of {{kells-hollow}}, she signed onto her first crew at twelve and bought her own hull at twenty-six. She carries an iron oath-ring she has never explained.

## Recent History

When {{house-veldrane}} seized her home port, Mara swore an Iron Vow to break their tariff. She has since crossed courses with {{captain-ferocious}} more than once — allies of convenience, never of trust.
